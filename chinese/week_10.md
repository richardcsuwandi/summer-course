# 💻 Week 10: Technology & Internet

## day 64

1.  科技 (kējì)
    *   **pinyin:** kējì
    *   **definition:** science and technology
    *   **usage:** the application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes, especially in industry. (repeated for emphasis in this theme)
    *   **example:** 科技的进步改变了我们的生活方式。(kējì de jìnbù gǎibiàn le wǒmen de shēnghuó fāngshì.) - the advancement of technology has changed our lifestyle.
    *   **contexts:** innovation, modern life, industry, research.

2.  互联网 (hùliánwǎng)
    *   **pinyin:** hùliánwǎng
    *   **definition:** internet
    *   **usage:** a global computer network providing a variety of information and communication facilities, consisting of interconnected networks using standardized communication protocols.
    *   **example:** 互联网已经成为信息传播的主要渠道。(hùliánwǎng yǐjīng chéngwéi xìnxī chuánbō de zhǔyào qúdào.) - the internet has become the main channel for information dissemination.
    *   **contexts:** communication, information, technology, daily life.

3.  网络 (wǎngluò)
    *   **pinyin:** wǎngluò
    *   **definition:** network (computer, social, etc.); internet
    *   **usage:** a group or system of interconnected people or things. often used interchangeably with 互联网 for internet.
    *   **example:** 我们公司的内部网络很安全。(wǒmen gōngsī de nèibù wǎngluò hěn ānquán.) - our company's internal network is very secure.
    *   **contexts:** technology, communication, computers, social connections.

4.  网站 (wǎngzhàn)
    *   **pinyin:** wǎngzhàn
    *   **definition:** website
    *   **usage:** a location connected to the internet that maintains one or more pages on the world wide web.
    *   **example:** 我经常浏览这个新闻网站。(wǒ jīngcháng liúlǎn zhège xīnwén wǎngzhàn.) - i often browse this news website.
    *   **contexts:** internet, information, e-commerce, entertainment.

5.  浏览器 (liúlǎnqì)
    *   **pinyin:** liúlǎnqì
    *   **definition:** browser (software)
    *   **usage:** a software application for accessing information on the world wide web.
    *   **example:** 你通常使用哪个浏览器？(nǐ tōngcháng shǐyòng nǎge liúlǎnqì?) - which browser do you usually use?
    *   **contexts:** internet, software, computers, web surfing.

6.  搜索引擎 (sōusuǒ yǐnqíng)
    *   **pinyin:** sōusuǒ yǐnqíng
    *   **definition:** search engine
    *   **usage:** a program that searches for and identifies items in a database that correspond to keywords or characters specified by the user, used especially for finding particular sites on the world wide web.
    *   **example:** 谷歌是一个非常流行的搜索引擎。(gǔgē shì yī gè fēicháng liúxíng de sōusuǒ yǐnqíng.) - google is a very popular search engine.
    *   **contexts:** internet, information retrieval, research.

7.  电子邮件 (diànzǐ yóujiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** diànzǐ yóujiàn
    *   **definition:** email
    *   **usage:** messages distributed by electronic means from one computer user to one or more recipients via a network.
    *   **example:** 请把文件通过电子邮件发给我。(qǐng bǎ wénjiàn tōngguò diànzǐ yóujiàn fā gěi wǒ.) - please send the file to me via email.
    *   **contexts:** communication, business, internet.

8.  下载 (xiàzài)
    *   **pinyin:** xiàzài
    *   **definition:** to download
    *   **usage:** copy (data) from one computer system to another, typically over the internet.
    *   **example:** 我需要下载这个软件的最新版本。(wǒ xūyào xiàzài zhège ruǎnjiàn de zuìxīn bǎnběn.) - i need to download the latest version of this software.
    *   **contexts:** internet, software, files, multimedia.

9.  上传 (shàngchuán)
    *   **pinyin:** shàngchuán
    *   **definition:** to upload
    *   **usage:** transfer (data) from one computer to another, typically to one that is larger or remote from the user or functioning as a server.
    *   **example:** 他把照片上传到了社交媒体。(tā bǎ zhàopiàn shàngchuán dàole shèjiāo méitǐ.) - he uploaded the photos to social media.
    *   **contexts:** internet, files, social media, cloud storage.

10. 用户名 (yònghùmíng)
    *   **pinyin:** yònghùmíng
    *   **definition:** username
    *   **usage:** an identification used by a person with access to a computer, network, or online service.
    *   **example:** 我忘了我的用户名是什么了。(wǒ wàng le wǒ de yònghùmíng shì shénme le.) - i forgot what my username is.
    *   **contexts:** internet accounts, login, software.

## day 65

1.  密码 (mìmǎ)
    *   **pinyin:** mìmǎ
    *   **definition:** password
    *   **usage:** a secret word or phrase that must be used to gain admission to something.
    *   **example:** 请设置一个复杂的密码以保护您的账户安全。(qǐng shèzhì yī gè fùzá de mìmǎ yǐ bǎohù nín de zhànghù ānquán.) - please set a complex password to protect your account security.
    *   **contexts:** internet accounts, login, security, privacy.

2.  账户 (zhànghù)
    *   **pinyin:** zhànghù
    *   **definition:** account (e.g., bank, online service)
    *   **usage:** a record or statement of financial expenditure and receipts relating to a particular period or purpose; an arrangement by which a user is given personalized access to a computer, website, or application.
    *   **example:** 我需要登录我的银行账户。(wǒ xūyào dēnglù wǒ de yínháng zhànghù.) - i need to log in to my bank account.
    *   **contexts:** finance, internet services, login.

3.  登录 (dēnglù)
    *   **pinyin:** dēnglù
    *   **definition:** to log in; to sign in
    *   **usage:** to go through the procedures to begin use of a computer system or online service.
    *   **example:** 请输入用户名和密码登录。(qǐng shūrù yònghùmíng hé mìmǎ dēnglù.) - please enter your username and password to log in.
    *   **contexts:** internet accounts, software, computers.

4.  注册 (zhùcè)
    *   **pinyin:** zhùcè
    *   **definition:** to register; to sign up
    *   **usage:** to enter or record on an official list or directory.
    *   **example:** 你需要先注册一个账户才能使用这项服务。(nǐ xūyào xiān zhùcè yī gè zhànghù cáinéng shǐyòng zhè xiàng fúwù.) - you need to register an account first to use this service.
    *   **contexts:** internet services, websites, applications, membership.

5.  软件 (ruǎnjiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** ruǎnjiàn
    *   **definition:** software
    *   **usage:** the programs and other operating information used by a computer.
    *   **example:** 我安装了一些新的办公软件。(wǒ ānzhuāng le yīxiē xīn de bàngōng ruǎnjiàn.) - i installed some new office software.
    *   **contexts:** computers, technology, applications.

6.  硬件 (yìngjiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** yìngjiàn
    *   **definition:** hardware (computer)
    *   **usage:** the machines, wiring, and other physical components of a computer or other electronic system.
    *   **example:** 这台电脑的硬件配置很高。(zhè tái diànnǎo de yìngjiàn pèizhì hěn gāo.) - the hardware configuration of this computer is very high.
    *   **contexts:** computers, technology, electronics.

7.  操作系统 (cāozuò xìtǒng)
    *   **pinyin:** cāozuò xìtǒng
    *   **definition:** operating system (OS)
    *   **usage:** the software that supports a computer's basic functions, such as scheduling tasks, executing applications, and controlling peripherals.
    *   **example:** Windows 和 macOS 是常见的操作系统。(windows hé macos shì chángjiàn de cāozuò xìtǒng.) - windows and macos are common operating systems.
    *   **contexts:** computers, software, technology.

8.  应用程序 (yìngyòng chéngxù)
    *   **pinyin:** yìngyòng chéngxù
    *   **definition:** application program; app
    *   **usage:** a program or piece of software designed and written to fulfill a particular purpose of the user.
    *   **example:** 我手机上安装了很多有用的应用程序。(wǒ shǒujī shàng ānzhuāng le hěnduō yǒuyòng de yìngyòng chéngxù.) - i have installed many useful apps on my phone.
    *   **contexts:** software, mobile devices, computers, technology.

9.  病毒 (bìngdú)
    *   **pinyin:** bìngdú
    *   **definition:** virus (computer or biological)
    *   **usage:** a piece of code which is capable of copying itself and typically has a detrimental effect, such as corrupting the system or destroying data; a microorganism that causes disease.
    *   **example:** 我的电脑中病毒了，需要杀毒。(wǒ de diànnǎo zhòng bìngdú le, xūyào shādú.) - my computer has a virus, i need to run an antivirus scan.
    *   **contexts:** computer security, health, cybersecurity.

10. 防火墙 (fánghuǒqiáng)
    *   **pinyin:** fánghuǒqiáng
    *   **definition:** firewall (computing)
    *   **usage:** a part of a computer system or network that is designed to block unauthorized access while permitting outward communication.
    *   **example:** 安装防火墙可以提高网络安全。(ānzhuāng fánghuǒqiáng kěyǐ tígāo wǎngluò ānquán.) - installing a firewall can improve network security.
    *   **contexts:** computer security, network, internet, cybersecurity.

## day 66

1.  数据库 (shùjùkù)
    *   **pinyin:** shùjùkù
    *   **definition:** database
    *   **usage:** a structured set of data held in a computer, especially one that is accessible in various ways.
    *   **example:** 这个公司拥有庞大的客户数据库。(zhège gōngsī yōngyǒu pángdà de kèhù shùjùkù.) - this company has a large customer database.
    *   **contexts:** computing, information technology, business, data management.

2.  服务器 (fúwùqì)
    *   **pinyin:** fúwùqì
    *   **definition:** server (computing)
    *   **usage:** a computer or computer program which manages access to a centralized resource or service in a network.
    *   **example:** 网站的数据存储在远程服务器上。(wǎngzhàn de shùjù cúnchǔ zài yuǎnchéng fúwùqì shàng.) - the website's data is stored on a remote server.
    *   **contexts:** computing, internet, networking, IT infrastructure.

3.  云计算 (yún jìsuàn)
    *   **pinyin:** yún jìsuàn
    *   **definition:** cloud computing
    *   **usage:** the practice of using a network of remote servers hosted on the internet to store, manage, and process data, rather than a local server or a personal computer.
    *   **example:** 云计算为企业提供了更灵活的数据存储方案。(yún jìsuàn wèi qǐyè tígōng le gèng línghuó de shùjù cúnchǔ fāng'àn.) - cloud computing provides businesses with more flexible data storage solutions.
    *   **contexts:** technology, internet, data storage, IT services.

4.  大数据 (dà shùjù)
    *   **pinyin:** dà shùjù
    *   **definition:** big data
    *   **usage:** extremely large data sets that may be analyzed computationally to reveal patterns, trends, and associations, especially relating to human behavior and interactions.
    *   **example:** 大数据分析在商业决策中越来越重要。(dà shùjù fēnxī zài shāngyè juécè zhōng yuèláiyuè zhòngyào.) - big data analysis is becoming increasingly important in business decision-making.
    *   **contexts:** technology, data analysis, business intelligence, research.

5.  人工智能 (réngōng zhìnéng)
    *   **pinyin:** réngōng zhìnéng
    *   **definition:** artificial intelligence (AI)
    *   **usage:** the theory and development of computer systems able to perform tasks that normally require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and translation between languages.
    *   **example:** 人工智能正在改变各个行业。(réngōng zhìnéng zhèngzài gǎibiàn gège hángyè.) - artificial intelligence is transforming various industries.
    *   **contexts:** technology, computer science, robotics, future trends.

6.  机器学习 (jīqì xuéxí)
    *   **pinyin:** jīqì xuéxí
    *   **definition:** machine learning
    *   **usage:** an application of artificial intelligence (AI) that provides systems the ability to automatically learn and improve from experience without being explicitly programmed.
    *   **example:** 机器学习是人工智能的一个重要分支。(jīqì xuéxí shì réngōng zhìnéng de yī gè zhòngyào fēnzhī.) - machine learning is an important branch of artificial intelligence.
    *   **contexts:** artificial intelligence, data science, computer science, technology.

7.  虚拟现实 (xūnǐ xiànshí)
    *   **pinyin:** xūnǐ xiànshí
    *   **definition:** virtual reality (VR)
    *   **usage:** the computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional image or environment that can be interacted with in a seemingly real or physical way by a person using special electronic equipment, such as a helmet with a screen inside or gloves fitted with sensors.
    *   **example:** 虚拟现实技术广泛应用于游戏和培训领域。(xūnǐ xiànshí jìshù guǎngfàn yìngyòng yú yóuxì hé péixùn lǐngyù.) - virtual reality technology is widely used in gaming and training fields.
    *   **contexts:** technology, gaming, simulation, entertainment, education.

8.  增强现实 (zēngqiáng xiànshí)
    *   **pinyin:** zēngqiáng xiànshí
    *   **definition:** augmented reality (AR)
    *   **usage:** a technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user's view of the real world, thus providing a composite view.
    *   **example:** 一些手机应用使用增强现实技术来提供互动体验。(yīxiē shǒujī yìngyòng shǐyòng zēngqiáng xiànshí jìshù lái tígōng hùdòng tǐyàn.) - some mobile apps use augmented reality technology to provide interactive experiences.
    *   **contexts:** technology, mobile apps, gaming, marketing.

9.  区块链 (qūkuàiliàn)
    *   **pinyin:** qūkuàiliàn
    *   **definition:** blockchain
    *   **usage:** a system in which a record of transactions made in bitcoin or another cryptocurrency are maintained across several computers that are linked in a peer-to-peer network.
    *   **example:** 区块链技术被认为是具有革命性的。(qūkuàiliàn jìshù bèi rènwéi shì jùyǒu gémìngxìng de.) - blockchain technology is considered revolutionary.
    *   **contexts:** cryptocurrency, finance, technology, data security.

10. 加密货币 (jiāmì huòbì)
    *   **pinyin:** jiāmì huòbì
    *   **definition:** cryptocurrency
    *   **usage:** a digital currency in which transactions are verified and records maintained by a decentralized system using cryptography, rather than by a centralized authority.
    *   **example:** 比特币是最早的加密货币之一。(bǐtèbì shì zuìzǎo de jiāmì huòbì zhīyī.) - bitcoin is one of the earliest cryptocurrencies.
    *   **contexts:** finance, technology, blockchain, investment.

## day 68

1.  程序员 (chéngxùyuán)
    *   **pinyin:** chéngxùyuán
    *   **definition:** programmer
    *   **usage:** a person who writes computer programs.
    *   **example:** 他立志成为一名优秀的程序员。(tā lìzhì chéngwéi yī míng yōuxiù de chéngxùyuán.) - he aspires to become an excellent programmer.
    *   **contexts:** technology, software development, IT industry, professions.

2.  工程师 (gōngchéngshī)
    *   **pinyin:** gōngchéngshī
    *   **definition:** engineer
    *   **usage:** a person who designs, builds, or maintains engines, machines, or public works.
    *   **example:** 这座桥梁是由一位著名工程师设计的。(zhè zuò qiáoliáng shì yóu yī wèi zhùmíng gōngchéngshī shèjì de.) - this bridge was designed by a famous engineer.
    *   **contexts:** technology, construction, manufacturing, professions.

3.  开发者 (kāifāzhě)
    *   **pinyin:** kāifāzhě
    *   **definition:** developer (e.g., software developer)
    *   **usage:** a person or company that creates new products, especially software or computer applications.
    *   **example:** 这款应用是由一个小型开发者团队制作的。(zhè kuǎn yìngyòng shì yóu yī gè xiǎoxíng kāifāzhě tuánduì zhìzuò de.) - this app was created by a small team of developers.
    *   **contexts:** software, technology, IT industry, game development.

4.  用户体验 (yònghù tǐyàn)
    *   **pinyin:** yònghù tǐyàn
    *   **definition:** user experience (UX)
    *   **usage:** the overall experience of a person using a product such as a website or computer application, especially in terms of how easy or pleasing it is to use.
    *   **example:** 好的用户体验对产品成功至关重要。(hǎo de yònghù tǐyàn duì chǎnpǐn chénggōng zhìguān zhòngyào.) - good user experience is crucial for product success.
    *   **contexts:** product design, web design, software development, marketing.

5.  用户界面 (yònghù jièmiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** yònghù jièmiàn
    *   **definition:** user interface (UI)
    *   **usage:** the means by which the user and a computer system interact, in particular the use of input devices and software.
    *   **example:** 这个软件的用户界面设计得很简洁。(zhège ruǎnjiàn de yònghù jièmiàn shèjì de hěn jiǎnjié.) - the user interface of this software is designed very concisely.
    *   **contexts:** software design, web design, app development, human-computer interaction.

6.  算法 (suànfǎ)
    *   **pinyin:** suànfǎ
    *   **definition:** algorithm
    *   **usage:** a process or set of rules to be followed in calculations or other problem-solving operations, especially by a computer.
    *   **example:** 搜索引擎使用复杂的算法对网页进行排序。(sōusuǒ yǐnqíng shǐyòng fùzá de suànfǎ duì wǎngyè jìnxíng páixù.) - search engines use complex algorithms to rank web pages.
    *   **contexts:** computer science, mathematics, data analysis, AI.

7.  代码 (dàimǎ)
    *   **pinyin:** dàimǎ
    *   **definition:** code (computer programming)
