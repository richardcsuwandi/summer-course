# ✉️ Week 11: Business Communication

## day 71

1.  会议 (huìyì)
    *   **pinyin:** huìyì
    *   **definition:** meeting; conference
    *   **usage:** an assembly of people, especially the members of a society or committee, for discussion or entertainment.
    *   **example:** 我们下午三点有一个重要会议。(wǒmen xiàwǔ sān diǎn yǒu yī gè zhòngyào huìyì.) - we have an important meeting at 3 pm.
    *   **contexts:** business, formal discussions, planning, decision-making.

2.  议程 (yìchéng)
    *   **pinyin:** yìchéng
    *   **definition:** agenda
    *   **usage:** a list of items to be discussed at a formal meeting.
    *   **example:** 请大家看一下今天的会议议程。(qǐng dàjiā kàn yīxià jīntiān de huìyì yìchéng.) - everyone, please take a look at today's meeting agenda.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, planning, organization, formal procedures.

3.  主持 (zhǔchí)
    *   **pinyin:** zhǔchí
    *   **definition:** to preside over; to host; to chair (a meeting)
    *   **usage:** to be in charge of a formal meeting, ceremony, or debate.
    *   **example:** 这次会议由王经理主持。(zhè cì huìyì yóu wáng jīnglǐ zhǔchí.) - this meeting will be chaired by manager wang.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, events, ceremonies, leadership.

4.  参与者 (cānyùzhě)
    *   **pinyin:** cānyùzhě
    *   **definition:** participant; attendee
    *   **usage:** a person who takes part in something.
    *   **example:** 请所有参与者准时出席。(qǐng suǒyǒu cānyùzhě zhǔnshí chūxí.) - all participants are requested to attend on time.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, events, activities, group work.

5.  发言 (fāyán)
    *   **pinyin:** fāyán
    *   **definition:** to make a speech; to speak (at a meeting); statement
    *   **usage:** to deliver a formal address or discourse to an audience.
    *   **example:** 他在会议上就预算问题发了言。(tā zài huìyì shàng jiù yùsuàn wèntí fā le yán.) - he spoke about the budget issue at the meeting.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, conferences, public speaking, discussions.

6.  讨论 (tǎolùn)
    *   **pinyin:** tǎolùn
    *   **definition:** to discuss; discussion
    *   **usage:** talk about (something) with another person or group of people.
    *   **example:** 我们需要深入讨论这个问题。(wǒmen xūyào shēnrù tǎolùn zhège wèntí.) - we need to discuss this issue in depth.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, problem-solving, academic settings, daily conversation.

7.  建议 (jiànyì)
    *   **pinyin:** jiànyì
    *   **definition:** to suggest; suggestion; proposal
    *   **usage:** put forward for consideration.
    *   **example:** 我建议我们推迟这个项目。(wǒ jiànyì wǒmen tuīchí zhège xiàngmù.) - i suggest we postpone this project.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, problem-solving, planning, daily interactions.

8.  决定 (juédìng)
    *   **pinyin:** juédìng
    *   **definition:** to decide; decision
    *   **usage:** make a choice from a number of alternatives.
    *   **example:** 公司决定投资这个新项目。(gōngsī juédìng tóuzī zhège xīn xiàngmù.) - the company decided to invest in this new project.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, planning, personal choices, management.

9.  会议纪要 (huìyì jìyào)
    *   **pinyin:** huìyì jìyào
    *   **definition:** minutes of a meeting
    *   **usage:** a written record of what was said and decided at a meeting.
    *   **example:** 请秘书整理一下会议纪要。(qǐng mìshū zhěnglǐ yīxià huìyì jìyào.) - please ask the secretary to prepare the meeting minutes.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, record-keeping, formal communication.

10. 演示 (yǎnshì)
    *   **pinyin:** yǎnshì
    *   **definition:** presentation; demonstration; to demonstrate
    *   **usage:** an act of showing or explaining how something works or is done.
    *   **example:** 他做了一个关于新产品的精彩演示。(tā zuò le yī gè guānyú xīn chǎnpǐn de jīngcǎi yǎnshì.) - he gave an excellent presentation about the new product.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, sales, education, product launches.

## day 72

1.  商务邮件 (shāngwù yóujiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** shāngwù yóujiàn
    *   **definition:** business email
    *   **usage:** email communication for professional or commercial purposes.
    *   **example:** 我每天都要处理很多商务邮件。(wǒ měitiān dōu yào chǔlǐ hěnduō shāngwù yóujiàn.) - i have to deal with many business emails every day.
    *   **contexts:** business communication, office work, professional correspondence.

2.  主题 (zhǔtí)
    *   **pinyin:** zhǔtí
    *   **definition:** subject (of an email); theme; topic
    *   **usage:** the matter or topic being discussed, written about, or studied. in email, it's the title line.
    *   **example:** 请在邮件主题中注明您的姓名和申请职位。(qǐng zài yóujiàn zhǔtí zhōng zhùmíng nín de xìngmíng hé shēnqǐng zhíwèi.) - please indicate your name and the position you are applying for in the email subject line.
    *   **contexts:** emails, writing, meetings, presentations.

3.  收件人 (shōujiànrén)
    *   **pinyin:** shōujiànrén
    *   **definition:** recipient (of an email, letter, etc.)
    *   **usage:** a person or thing that receives or is awarded something.
    *   **example:** 请确认收件人地址是否正确。(qǐng quèrèn shōujiànrén dìzhǐ shìfǒu zhèngquè.) - please confirm if the recipient's address is correct.
    *   **contexts:** email, mail, deliveries.

4.  发件人 (fājiànrén)
    *   **pinyin:** fājiànrén
    *   **definition:** sender (of an email, letter, etc.)
    *   **usage:** a person who sends or transmits a message, letter, or parcel.
    *   **example:** 这封邮件的发件人是谁？(zhè fēng yóujiàn de fājiànrén shì shéi?) - who is the sender of this email?
    *   **contexts:** email, mail, communication.

5.  抄送 (chāosòng)
    *   **pinyin:** chāosòng
    *   **definition:** cc (carbon copy); to send a carbon copy
    *   **usage:** to send a copy of a letter or email to someone in addition to the primary recipient.
    *   **example:** 请把这封邮件抄送给相关部门的同事。(qǐng bǎ zhè fēng yóujiàn chāosòng gěi xiāngguān bùmén de tóngshì.) - please cc this email to colleagues in relevant departments.
    *   **contexts:** email, office communication, correspondence.

6.  密送 (mìsòng)
    *   **pinyin:** mìsòng
    *   **definition:** bcc (blind carbon copy); to send a blind carbon copy
    *   **usage:** to send a copy of an email to someone without the other recipients knowing.
    *   **example:** 为了保护隐私，我把客户的邮箱地址放在了密送栏。(wèile bǎohù yǐnsī, wǒ bǎ kèhù de yóuxiāng dìzhǐ fàng zài le mìsòng lán.) - to protect privacy, i put the client's email address in the bcc field.
    *   **contexts:** email, privacy, office communication.

7.  附件 (fùjiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** fùjiàn
    *   **definition:** attachment (email)
    *   **usage:** a computer file sent with an email message.
    *   **example:** 请查收邮件附件中的报告。(qǐng cháshōu yóujiàn fùjiàn zhōng de bàogào.) - please check the report in the email attachment.
    *   **contexts:** email, file sharing, documents.

8.  回复 (huífù)
    *   **pinyin:** huífù
    *   **definition:** to reply; reply (noun)
    *   **usage:** say something in response to something someone has said or written.
    *   **example:** 我会尽快回复您的邮件。(wǒ huì jǐnkuài huífù nín de yóujiàn.) - i will reply to your email as soon as possible.
    *   **contexts:** email, letters, messages, communication.

9.  转发 (zhuǎnfā)
    *   **pinyin:** zhuǎnfā
    *   **definition:** to forward (an email, message)
    *   **usage:** to send on (a letter or email that has been received) to a new address or recipient.
    *   **example:** 我把你的邮件转发给老板了。(wǒ bǎ nǐ de yóujiàn zhuǎnfā gěi lǎobǎn le.) - i forwarded your email to the boss.
    *   **contexts:** email, information sharing, communication.

10. 签名 (qiānmíng)
    *   **pinyin:** qiānmíng
    *   **definition:** signature (in an email or on a document); to sign one's name
    *   **usage:** a person's name written in a distinctive way as a form of identification in authorizing a document or concluding a letter; a personalized block of text automatically appended at the bottom of an email message.
    *   **example:** 别忘了在邮件末尾加上你的电子签名。(bié wàng le zài yóujiàn mòwěi jiāshàng nǐ de diànzǐ qiānmíng.) - don't forget to add your email signature at the end of the email.
    *   **contexts:** email, documents, legal matters, personal identification.

## day 73

1.  正式 (zhèngshì)
    *   **pinyin:** zhèngshì
    *   **definition:** formal; official
    *   **usage:** done in accordance with rules of convention or etiquette; suitable for or constituting an official or important occasion.
    *   **example:** 这是一封正式的商务邀请函。(zhè shì yī fēng zhèngshì de shāngwù yāoqǐnghán.) - this is a formal business invitation letter.
    *   **contexts:** business communication, official documents, ceremonies, dress code.

2.  非正式 (fēizhèngshì)
    *   **pinyin:** fēizhèngshì
    *   **definition:** informal; unofficial
    *   **usage:** having a relaxed, friendly, or unofficial style, manner, or nature.
    *   **example:** 我们可以进行一次非正式的讨论。(wǒmen kěyǐ jìnxíng yī cì fēizhèngshì de tǎolùn.) - we can have an informal discussion.
    *   **contexts:** daily conversation, casual meetings, social gatherings.

3.  开场白 (kāichǎngbái)
    *   **pinyin:** kāichǎngbái
    *   **definition:** opening remarks; introduction (in a speech or meeting)
    *   **usage:** the first part of a speech or presentation, intended to engage the audience and introduce the topic.
    *   **example:** 他的开场白很有吸引力。(tā de kāichǎngbái hěn yǒu xīyǐnlì.) - his opening remarks were very engaging.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, presentations, speeches, formal events.

4.  总结 (zǒngjié)
    *   **pinyin:** zǒngjié
    *   **definition:** to summarize; summary; conclusion
    *   **usage:** give a brief statement of the main points of (something).
    *   **example:** 会议结束前，请允许我做个总结。(huìyì jiéshù qián, qǐng yǔnxǔ wǒ zuò gè zǒngjié.) - before the meeting ends, please allow me to make a summary.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, reports, essays, presentations.

5.  后续行动 (hòuxù xíngdòng)
    *   **pinyin:** hòuxù xíngdòng
    *   **definition:** follow-up action; next steps
    *   **usage:** further action connected with something that has happened or been discussed previously.
    *   **example:** 我们需要确定下一步的后续行动。(wǒmen xūyào quèdìng xià yī bù de hòuxù xíngdòng.) - we need to determine the follow-up actions for the next step.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, project management, planning.

6.  截止日期 (jiézhǐ rìqī)
    *   **pinyin:** jiézhǐ rìqī
    *   **definition:** deadline; due date
    *   **usage:** the latest time or date by which something should be completed.
    *   **example:** 这个项目的截止日期是下周五。(zhège xiàngmù de jiézhǐ rìqī shì xiàzhōu wǔ.) - the deadline for this project is next friday.
    *   **contexts:** project management, work, assignments, applications.

7.  协商 (xiéshāng)
    *   **pinyin:** xiéshāng
    *   **definition:** to negotiate; negotiation; to consult
    *   **usage:** discussion aimed at reaching an agreement.
    *   **example:** 双方正在就合同条款进行协商。(shuāngfāng zhèngzài jiù hétong tiáokuǎn jìnxíng xiéshāng.) - both parties are negotiating the terms of the contract.
    *   **contexts:** business deals, contracts, conflict resolution, diplomacy.

8.  合作 (hézuò)
    *   **pinyin:** hézuò
    *   **definition:** to cooperate; cooperation; collaboration
    *   **usage:** the action or process of working together to the same end.
    *   **example:** 我们期待与贵公司有更多的合作机会。(wǒmen qīdài yǔ guì gōngsī yǒu gèng duō de hézuò jīhuì.) - we look forward to more cooperation opportunities with your company.
    *   **contexts:** business, teamwork, projects, international relations.

9.  确认 (quèrèn)
    *   **pinyin:** què rèn
    *   **definition:** to confirm; confirmation; to verify
    *   **usage:** establish the truth or correctness of (something previously believed, suspected, or feared to be the case).
    *   **example:** 请确认您是否收到了我的邮件。(qǐng quèrèn nín shìfǒu shōudào le wǒ de yóujiàn.) - please confirm whether you have received my email.
    *   **contexts:** appointments, reservations, information, communication.

10. 通知 (tōngzhī)
    *   **pinyin:** tōngzhī
    *   **definition:** to notify; to inform; notice; notification
    *   **usage:** information or a warning given in advance of something impending.
    *   **example:** 公司发出通知，下周一放假。(gōngsī fāchū tōngzhī, xiàzhōu yī fàngjià.) - the company issued a notice that next monday is a holiday.
    *   **contexts:** announcements, official communication, information sharing.

## day 74

1.  安排 (ānpái)
    *   **pinyin:** ānpái
    *   **definition:** to arrange; arrangement; to plan
    *   **usage:** make preparations for (a future event or activity).
    *   **example:** 我会安排一次会议讨论这个方案。(wǒ huì ānpái yī cì huìyì tǎolùn zhège fāng'àn.) - i will arrange a meeting to discuss this plan.
    *   **contexts:** scheduling, planning, events, daily tasks.

2.  日程 (rìchéng)
    *   **pinyin:** rìchéng
    *   **definition:** schedule; itinerary
    *   **usage:** a plan for carrying out a process or procedure, giving lists of intended events and times.
    *   **example:** 请把您的日程发给我。(qǐng bǎ nín de rìchéng fā gěi wǒ.) - please send me your schedule.
    *   **contexts:** work, travel, appointments, planning.

3.  预约 (yùyuē)
    *   **pinyin:** yùyuē
    *   **definition:** to make an appointment; appointment; reservation
    *   **usage:** an arrangement to meet someone or be somewhere at a particular time.
    *   **example:** 我想预约明天下午和李医生见面。(wǒ xiǎng yùyuē míngtiān xiàwǔ hé lǐ yīshēng jiànmiàn.) - i would like to make an appointment to see dr. li tomorrow afternoon.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, doctor's visits, services, restaurants.

4.  取消 (qǔxiāo)
    *   **pinyin:** qǔxiāo
    *   **definition:** to cancel; cancellation
    *   **usage:** decide that an arranged event will not take place.
    *   **example:** 由于天气原因，会议被迫取消。(yóuyú tiānqì yuányīn, huìyì bèipò qǔxiāo.) - due to weather reasons, the meeting had to be cancelled.
    *   **contexts:** appointments, reservations, events, orders.

5.  推迟 (tuīchí)
    *   **pinyin:** tuīchí
    *   **definition:** to postpone; to put off; to delay
    *   **usage:** cause or arrange for (something) to take place at a time later than that first scheduled.
    *   **example:** 我们不得不把会议推迟到下周。(wǒmen bùdébù bǎ huìyì tuīchí dào xiàzhōu.) - we have to postpone the meeting until next week.
    *   **contexts:** meetings, events, deadlines, travel plans.

6.  提前 (tíqián)
    *   **pinyin:** tíqián
    *   **definition:** to advance (a date, time); to do sth in advance; ahead of schedule
    *   **usage:** done, happening, or turned in before the customary or expected time.
    *   **example:** 我们可以把会议提前到上午吗？(wǒmen kěyǐ bǎ huìyì tíqián dào shàngwǔ ma?) - can we move the meeting earlier to the morning?
    *   **contexts:** scheduling, appointments, deadlines.

7.  跟进 (gēnjìn)
    *   **pinyin:** gēnjìn
    *   **definition:** to follow up; follow-up
    *   **usage:** to pursue or investigate something further.
    *   **example:** 请销售团队跟进一下这个客户。(qǐng xiāoshòu tuánduì gēnjìn yīxià zhège kèhù.) - please have the sales team follow up with this client.
    *   **contexts:** sales, customer service, project management, tasks.

8.  反馈 (fǎnkuì)
    *   **pinyin:** fǎnkuì
    *   **definition:** feedback
    *   **usage:** information about reactions to a product, a person's performance of a task, etc. which is used as a basis for improvement.
    *   **example:** 我们非常重视客户的反馈意见。(wǒmen fēicháng zhòngshì kèhù de fǎnkuì yìjiàn.) - we attach great importance to customer feedback.
    *   **contexts:** performance reviews, product development, customer service, surveys.

9.  询问 (xúnwèn)
    *   **pinyin:** xúnwèn
    *   **definition:** to inquire; inquiry
    *   **usage:** ask for information from someone.
    *   **example:** 我想询问一下关于这个产品的信息。(wǒ xiǎng xúnwèn yīxià guānyú zhège chǎnpǐn de xìnxī.) - i would like to inquire about information regarding this product.
    *   **contexts:** customer service, information gathering, formal requests.

10. 答复 (dáfù)
    *   **pinyin:** dáfù
    *   **definition:** to reply; to answer; reply (formal)
    *   **usage:** a spoken or written answer. often used in more formal contexts than 回复.
    *   **example:** 我们会在三个工作日内给您明确答复。(wǒmen huì zài sān gè gōngzuòrì nèi gěi nín míngquè dáfù.) - we will give you a clear reply within three working days.
    *   **contexts:** formal correspondence, official inquiries, customer service.

## day 75

1.  商业伙伴 (shāngyè huǒbàn)
    *   **pinyin:** shāngyè huǒbàn
    *   **definition:** business partner
    *   **usage:** a commercial entity with which another commercial entity has some form of alliance.
    *   **example:** 我们正在寻找新的商业伙伴。(wǒmen zhèngzài xúnzhǎo xīn de shāngyè huǒbàn.) - we are looking for new business partners.
    *   **contexts:** business development, partnerships, collaboration.

2.  客户 (kèhù)
    *   **pinyin:** kèhù
    *   **definition:** client; customer
    *   **usage:** a person or organization using the services of a lawyer or other professional person or company.
    *   **example:** 满足客户的需求是我们的首要任务。(mǎnzú kèhù de xūqiú shì wǒmen de shǒuyào rènwù.) - meeting the needs of our clients is our top priority.
    *   **contexts:** business, sales, services, marketing.

3.  供应商 (gōngyìngshāng)
    *   **pinyin:** gōngyìngshāng
    *   **definition:** supplier; vendor
    *   **usage:** a person or organization that provides something needed such as a product or service.
    *   **example:** 我们需要找到可靠的原材料供应商。(wǒmen xūyào zhǎodào kěkào de yuáncáiliào gōngyìngshāng.) - we need to find reliable raw material suppliers.
    *   **contexts:** business, procurement, manufacturing, supply chain.

4.  合同 (hétong)
    *   **pinyin:** hétong
    *   **definition:** contract; agreement
    *   **usage:** a written or spoken agreement, especially one concerning employment, sales, or tenancy, that is intended to be enforceable by law.
    *   **example:** 请仔细阅读合同条款。(qǐng zǐxì yuèdú hétong tiáokuǎn.) - please read the contract terms carefully.
    *   **contexts:** business, legal matters, employment, real estate.

5.  条款 (tiáokuǎn)
    *   **pinyin:** tiáokuǎn
    *   **definition:** clause; term (of a contract or agreement)
    *   **usage:** a particular and separate article, stipulation, or proviso in a legal document.
    *   **example:** 我们需要讨论一下合同中的付款条款。(wǒmen xūyào tǎolùn yīxià hétong zhōng de fùkuǎn tiáokuǎn.) - we need to discuss the payment terms in the contract.
    *   **contexts:** contracts, legal documents, agreements.

6.  谈判 (tánpàn)
    *   **pinyin:** tánpàn
    *   **definition:** to negotiate; negotiation
    *   **usage:** discussion aimed at reaching an agreement. (similar to 协商 but can imply more formal or high-stakes situations)
    *   **example:** 经过几轮谈判，双方终于达成了协议。(jīngguò jǐ lún tánpàn, shuāngfāng zhōngyú dáchéng le xiéyì.) - after several rounds of negotiations, both sides finally reached an agreement.
    *   **contexts:** business deals, diplomacy, conflict resolution, legal settlements.

7.  协议 (xiéyì)
    *   **pinyin:** xiéyì
    *   **definition:** agreement; pact; protocol
    *   **usage:** a formal agreement between two or more parties.
    *   **example:** 双方签署了合作协议。(shuāngfāng qiānshǔ le hézuò xiéyì.) - both parties signed a cooperation agreement.
    *   **contexts:** business, legal matters, international relations.

8.  报价 (bàojià)
    *   **pinyin:** bàojià
    *   **definition:** to quote (a price); quotation
    *   **usage:** a formal statement setting out the estimated cost for a particular job or service.
    *   **example:** 请给我们一份详细的产品报价单。(qǐng gěi wǒmen yī fèn xiángxì de chǎnpǐn bàojiàdān.) - please give us a detailed product quotation list.
    *   **contexts:** sales, procurement, business transactions, services.

9.  订单 (dìngdān)
    *   **pinyin:** dìngdān
    *   **definition:** order (for goods); purchase order
    *   **usage:** a confirmed request by one party to another to buy, sell, deliver, or receive goods or services under specified terms and conditions.
    *   **example:** 我们收到了一份大额订单。(wǒmen shōudào le yī fèn dà'é dìngdān.) - we received a large order.
    *   **contexts:** sales, e-commerce, manufacturing, logistics.

10. 发票 (fāpiào)
    *   **pinyin:** fāpiào
    *   **definition:** invoice; receipt (official)
    *   **usage:** a list of goods sent or services provided, with a statement of the sum due for these; an official document for tax or reimbursement purposes.
    *   **example:** 请开一张发票给我。(qǐng kāi yī zhāng fāpiào gěi wǒ.) - please issue an invoice to me.
    *   **contexts:** business transactions, accounting, expenses, reimbursement.

## day 76

1.  市场营销 (shìchǎng yíngxiāo)
    *   **pinyin:** shìchǎng yíngxiāo
