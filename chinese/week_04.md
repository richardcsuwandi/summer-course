# ✈️ Week 4: Travel & Transportation

## day 22

1.  旅行 (lǚxíng)
    *   **pinyin:** lǚxíng
    *   **definition:** to travel; journey; trip
    *   **usage:** making a journey, typically of some length or abroad.
    *   **example:** 我喜欢独自旅行。(wǒ xǐhuān dúzì lǚxíng.) - i like to travel alone.
    *   **contexts:** travel, holidays, leisure.

2.  目的地 (mùdìdì)
    *   **pinyin:** mùdìdì
    *   **definition:** destination
    *   **usage:** the place to which someone or something is going or being sent.
    *   **example:** 我们下一个目的地是哪里？(wǒmen xià yī gè mùdìdì shì nǎlǐ?) - where is our next destination?
    *   **contexts:** travel, navigation, logistics.

3.  行程 (xíngchéng)
    *   **pinyin:** xíngchéng
    *   **definition:** itinerary; route; journey
    *   **usage:** a planned route or journey.
    *   **example:** 请确认一下我们的行程安排。(qǐng quèrèn yīxià wǒmen de xíngchéng ānpái.) - please confirm our itinerary arrangements.
    *   **contexts:** travel planning, tours, business trips.

4.  护照 (hùzhào)
    *   **pinyin:** hùzhào
    *   **definition:** passport
    *   **usage:** an official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries.
    *   **example:** 出国旅行别忘了带护照。(chūguó lǚxíng bié wàng le dài hùzhào.) - don't forget to bring your passport when traveling abroad.
    *   **contexts:** international travel, official documents.

5.  签证 (qiānzhèng)
    *   **pinyin:** qiānzhèng
    *   **definition:** visa
    *   **usage:** an endorsement on a passport indicating that the holder is allowed to enter, leave, or stay for a specified period of time in a country.
    *   **example:** 我需要办理去美国的签证。(wǒ xūyào bànlǐ qù měiguó de qiānzhèng.) - i need to apply for a visa to the united states.
    *   **contexts:** international travel, immigration, official documents.

6.  机票 (jīpiào)
    *   **pinyin:** jīpiào
    *   **definition:** plane ticket; air ticket
    *   **usage:** a ticket for a journey on an aircraft.
    *   **example:** 我已经预订了往返机票。(wǒ yǐjīng yùdìng le wǎngfǎn jīpiào.) - i have already booked a round-trip plane ticket.
    *   **contexts:** air travel, booking, transportation.

7.  航班 (hángbān)
    *   **pinyin:** hángbān
    *   **definition:** scheduled flight; flight number
    *   **usage:** a journey made by air, especially in a plane; the particular flight identified by its number.
    *   **example:** 我们的航班延误了。(wǒmen de hángbān yánwù le.) - our flight has been delayed.
    *   **contexts:** air travel, airports, flight information.

8.  机场 (jīchǎng)
    *   **pinyin:** jīchǎng
    *   **definition:** airport
    *   **usage:** a complex of runways and buildings for the takeoff, landing, accommodation, and maintenance of aircraft, with facilities for passengers and cargo.
    *   **example:** 我们需要提前三个小时到达机场。(wǒmen xūyào tíqián sān gè xiǎoshí dàodá jīchǎng.) - we need to arrive at the airport three hours in advance.
    *   **contexts:** air travel, transportation hubs.

9.  登机口 (dēngjīkǒu)
    *   **pinyin:** dēngjīkǒu
    *   **definition:** boarding gate (at an airport)
    *   **usage:** the gate in an airport where passengers board their aircraft.
    *   **example:** 请问飞往北京的航班在哪个登机口？(qǐngwèn fēi wǎng běijīng de hángbān zài nǎge dēngjīkǒu?) - excuse me, which boarding gate is for the flight to beijing?
    *   **contexts:** airports, air travel.

10. 行李 (xíngli)
    *   **pinyin:** xíngli
    *   **definition:** luggage; baggage
    *   **usage:** cases and bags used to carry belongings when traveling.
    *   **example:** 我的行李超重了。(wǒ de xíngli chāozhòng le.) - my luggage is overweight.
    *   **contexts:** travel, airports, packing.

## day 23

1.  托运 (tuōyùn)
    *   **pinyin:** tuōyùn
    *   **definition:** to check in (luggage); to consign for shipment
    *   **usage:** to hand over luggage to an airline to be transported in the cargo hold of an aircraft.
    *   **example:** 我需要托运这件行李。(wǒ xūyào tuōyùn zhè jiàn xíngli.) - i need to check in this piece of luggage.
    *   **contexts:** airports, air travel, luggage.

2.  随身携带 (suíshēn xiédài)
    *   **pinyin:** suíshēn xiédài
    *   **definition:** to carry on (luggage); to bring with oneself
    *   **usage:** refers to luggage or items that a passenger is allowed to carry aboard an aircraft or other vehicle.
    *   **example:** 这件小行李我可以随身携带。(zhè jiàn xiǎo xíngli wǒ kěyǐ suíshēn xiédài.) - i can carry this small piece of luggage on board.
    *   **contexts:** air travel, luggage regulations, security.

3.  安检 (ānjiǎn)
    *   **pinyin:** ānjiǎn
    *   **definition:** security check
    *   **usage:** an examination of a person or their belongings to ensure they are not carrying dangerous or prohibited items, especially at an airport.
    *   **example:** 我们需要通过安检才能登机。(wǒmen xūyào tōngguò ānjiǎn cáinéng dēngjī.) - we need to go through the security check before boarding.
    *   **contexts:** airports, security, travel procedures.

4.  海关 (hǎiguān)
    *   **pinyin:** hǎiguān
    *   **definition:** customs (border control)
    *   **usage:** the official department that administers and collects the duties levied by a government on imported goods.
    *   **example:** 入境时需要通过海关检查。(rùjìng shí xūyào tōngguò hǎiguān jiǎnchá.) - you need to pass through customs inspection upon entry.
    *   **contexts:** international travel, borders, government.

5.  酒店 (jiǔdiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** jiǔdiàn
    *   **definition:** hotel; large restaurant (sometimes)
    *   **usage:** an establishment providing accommodation, meals, and other services for travelers and tourists. can sometimes also refer to a large, often high-end, restaurant.
    *   **example:** 我已经预订了市中心的酒店。(wǒ yǐjīng yùdìng le shìzhōngxīn de jiǔdiàn.) - i have already booked a hotel in the city center.
    *   **contexts:** travel, accommodation, hospitality.

6.  预订 (yùdìng)
    *   **pinyin:** yùdìng
    *   **definition:** to reserve; to book (a ticket, a hotel room, etc.)
    *   **usage:** to arrange for (a room, seat, ticket, etc.) to be kept for one's use.
    *   **example:** 你预订机票和酒店了吗？(nǐ yùdìng jīpiào hé jiǔdiàn le ma?) - have you booked your flight ticket and hotel?
    *   **contexts:** travel planning, accommodation, tickets.

7.  入住 (rùzhù)
    *   **pinyin:** rùzhù
    *   **definition:** to check in (at a hotel)
    *   **usage:** to arrive and register at a hotel or guesthouse.
    *   **example:** 我们下午三点可以办理入住手续。(wǒmen xiàwǔ sāndiǎn kěyǐ bànlǐ rùzhù shǒuxù.) - we can check in at 3 pm.
    *   **contexts:** hotels, accommodation, travel procedures.

8.  退房 (tuìfáng)
    *   **pinyin:** tuìfáng
    *   **definition:** to check out (of a hotel)
    *   **usage:** to settle one's bill and leave a hotel.
    *   **example:** 明天中午十二点前必须退房。(míngtiān zhōngwǔ shí'èr diǎn qián bìxū tuìfáng.) - we must check out before 12 noon tomorrow.
    *   **contexts:** hotels, accommodation, travel procedures.

9.  交通 (jiāotōng)
    *   **pinyin:** jiāotōng
    *   **definition:** transportation; traffic
    *   **usage:** the system or means of conveying people or goods from place to place; vehicles moving on a road or in an area.
    *   **example:** 这个城市的交通很方便。(zhège chéngshì de jiāotōng hěn fāngbiàn.) - the transportation in this city is very convenient.
    *   **contexts:** urban life, travel, logistics.

10. 公共交通 (gōnggòng jiāotōng)
    *   **pinyin:** gōnggòng jiāotōng
    *   **definition:** public transportation
    *   **usage:** buses, trains, subways, and other forms of transport that are available to the public, charge set fares, and run on fixed routes.
    *   **example:** 我通常乘坐公共交通上下班。(wǒ tōngcháng chéngzuò gōnggòng jiāotōng shàngxiàbān.) - i usually take public transportation to commute.
    *   **contexts:** urban life, travel, environment.

## day 24

1.  地铁 (dìtiě)
    *   **pinyin:** dìtiě
    *   **definition:** subway; metro; underground railway
    *   **usage:** an underground electric railway.
    *   **example:** 坐地铁去市中心很快。(zuò dìtiě qù shìzhōngxīn hěn kuài.) - taking the subway to the city center is very fast.
    *   **contexts:** urban transportation, daily commute.

2.  公交车 (gōngjiāochē)
    *   **pinyin:** gōngjiāochē
    *   **definition:** public bus
    *   **usage:** a large motor vehicle carrying passengers by road, typically one serving the public on a fixed route and for a set fare.
    *   **example:** 我每天坐公交车上学。(wǒ měitiān zuò gōngjiāochē shàngxué.) - i take the public bus to school every day.
    *   **contexts:** urban transportation, daily commute.

3.  出租车 (chūzūchē)
    *   **pinyin:** chūzūchē
    *   **definition:** taxi; cab
    *   **usage:** a car licensed to transport passengers in return for payment of a fare.
    *   **example:** 如果赶时间，我们可以打出租车。(rúguǒ gǎn shíjiān, wǒmen kěyǐ dǎ chūzūchē.) - if we are in a hurry, we can take a taxi.
    *   **contexts:** transportation, convenience.

4.  网约车 (wǎngyuēchē)
    *   **pinyin:** wǎngyuēchē
    *   **definition:** ride-hailing service car (e.g., didi, uber)
    *   **usage:** a car service requested via a mobile app.
    *   **example:** 现在叫网约车很方便。(xiànzài jiào wǎngyuēchē hěn fāngbiàn.) - it's very convenient to hail a ride online now.
    *   **contexts:** transportation, technology, convenience.

5.  火车 (huǒchē)
    *   **pinyin:** huǒchē
    *   **definition:** train
    *   **usage:** a series of railroad cars moved as a unit by a locomotive or by integral motors.
    *   **example:** 我计划坐火车去邻近的城市。(wǒ jìhuà zuò huǒchē qù línjìn de chéngshì.) - i plan to take a train to the neighboring city.
    *   **contexts:** long-distance travel, transportation.

6.  高铁 (gāotiě)
    *   **pinyin:** gāotiě
    *   **definition:** high-speed rail
    *   **usage:** a type of rail transport that operates significantly faster than traditional rail traffic, using an integrated system of specialized rolling stock and dedicated tracks.
    *   **example:** 乘坐高铁既快速又舒适。(chéngzuò gāotiě jì kuàisù yòu shūshì.) - traveling by high-speed rail is both fast and comfortable.
    *   **contexts:** modern transportation, long-distance travel, china.

7.  车站 (chēzhàn)
    *   **pinyin:** chēzhàn
    *   **definition:** station (bus or train)
    *   **usage:** a building where buses or trains regularly stop, allowing passengers to board or alight.
    *   **example:** 火车站在哪里？(huǒchēzhàn zài nǎlǐ?) - where is the train station?
    *   **contexts:** transportation hubs, travel.

8.  售票处 (shòupiàochù)
    *   **pinyin:** shòupiàochù
    *   **definition:** ticket office; booking office
    *   **usage:** a place where tickets are sold.
    *   **example:** 你可以去售票处买票。(nǐ kěyǐ qù shòupiàochù mǎipiào.) - you can go to the ticket office to buy tickets.
    *   **contexts:** train stations, airports, cinemas, events.

9.  候车室 (hòuchēshì)
    *   **pinyin:** hòuchēshì
    *   **definition:** waiting room (for a train or bus)
    *   **usage:** a room provided for the use of people waiting for a train or bus.
    *   **example:** 我们在候车室等火车。(wǒmen zài hòuchēshì děng huǒchē.) - we are waiting for the train in the waiting room.
    *   **contexts:** train stations, bus stations.

10. 站台 (zhàntái)
    *   **pinyin:** zhàntái
    *   **definition:** platform (at a train or subway station)
    *   **usage:** a raised level surface alongside a railway track where passengers can board and alight from trains.
    *   **example:** 请在三号站台等候列车。(qǐng zài sānhào zhàntái děnghòu lièchē.) - please wait for the train on platform three.
    *   **contexts:** train stations, subway stations.

10. 纪念品 (jìniànpǐn)

    *   **example:** 我还在倒时差。(wǒ hái zài dǎo shíchā.) - i am still adjusting to the jet lag.
    *   **contexts:** international travel, time zones, health.

10. 迷路 (mílù)
    *   **pinyin:** mílù
    *   **definition:** to get lost; to lose one's way
    *   **usage:** to be unable to find one's way; not knowing one's whereabouts.
    *   **example:** 我在陌生的城市迷路了。(wǒ zài mòshēng de chéngshì mílù le.) - i got lost in an unfamiliar city.
    *   **contexts:** travel, navigation, directions.
