# 🛍️ Week 5: Shopping & Making Purchases

## day 29

1.  购物 (gòuwù)
    *   **pinyin:** gòuwù
    *   **definition:** shopping; to shop
    *   **usage:** the activity of buying goods from shops.
    *   **example:** 我周末喜欢去购物中心购物。(wǒ zhōumò xǐhuān qù gòuwù zhōngxīn gòuwù.) - i like to go shopping at the mall on weekends.
    *   **contexts:** leisure, errands, commerce.

2.  商店 (shāngdiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** shāngdiàn
    *   **definition:** shop; store
    *   **usage:** a building or part of a building where goods or services are sold.
    *   **example:** 这条街上有很多小商店。(zhè tiáo jiē shàng yǒu hěnduō xiǎo shāngdiàn.) - there are many small shops on this street.
    *   **contexts:** commerce, daily life, urban environment.

3.  商场 (shāngchǎng)
    *   **pinyin:** shāngchǎng
    *   **definition:** shopping mall; department store
    *   **usage:** a large enclosed shopping area from which traffic is excluded; a large store stocking a wide variety of goods in different departments.
    *   **example:** 这家商场正在打折促销。(zhè jiā shāngchǎng zhèngzài dǎzhé cùxiāo.) - this shopping mall is having a discount promotion.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, retail, leisure.

4.  超市 (chāoshì)
    *   **pinyin:** chāoshì
    *   **definition:** supermarket
    *   **usage:** a large self-service store selling groceries and household goods.
    *   **example:** 我要去超市买些牛奶和面包。(wǒ yào qù chāoshì mǎi xiē niúnǎi hé miànbāo.) - i need to go to the supermarket to buy some milk and bread.
    *   **contexts:** grocery shopping, daily errands.

5.  市场 (shìchǎng)
    *   **pinyin:** shìchǎng
    *   **definition:** market (place)
    *   **usage:** a regular gathering of people for the purchase and sale of provisions, livestock, and other commodities.
    *   **example:** 菜市场的水果很新鲜。(cài shìchǎng de shuǐguǒ hěn xīnxiān.) - the fruits at the wet market are very fresh.
    *   **contexts:** shopping (especially for fresh produce), local culture, commerce.

6.  网上购物 (wǎngshàng gòuwù)
    *   **pinyin:** wǎngshàng gòuwù
    *   **definition:** online shopping
    *   **usage:** the action or activity of buying goods or services over the internet.
    *   **example:** 网上购物越来越流行了。(wǎngshàng gòuwù yuèláiyuè liúxíng le.) - online shopping is becoming increasingly popular.
    *   **contexts:** e-commerce, internet, modern lifestyle.

7.  淘宝 (táobǎo)
    *   **pinyin:** táobǎo
    *   **definition:** taobao (popular chinese online shopping website)
    *   **usage:** refers specifically to the taobao e-commerce platform, but can sometimes be used more generally to mean online shopping in china.
    *   **example:** 我经常在淘宝上买东西。(wǒ jīngcháng zài táobǎo shàng mǎi dōngxi.) - i often buy things on taobao.
    *   **contexts:** online shopping, e-commerce in china.

8.  购物车 (gòuwùchē)
    *   **pinyin:** gòuwùchē
    *   **definition:** shopping cart (physical or online)
    *   **usage:** a cart supplied by a store for use by customers for carrying merchandise to the checkout counter; an icon on an e-commerce website representing a virtual cart for items a user intends to purchase.
    *   **example:** 我的购物车里已经有很多东西了。(wǒ de gòuwùchē lǐ yǐjīng yǒu hěnduō dōngxi le.) - my shopping cart is already full of things.
    *   **contexts:** shopping (in-store or online), e-commerce.

9.  试穿 (shìchuān)
    *   **pinyin:** shìchuān
    *   **definition:** to try on (clothes, shoes)
    *   **usage:** to put on an article of clothing to see if it fits or is suitable.
    *   **example:** 我可以试穿这件衬衫吗？(wǒ kěyǐ shìchuān zhè jiàn chènshān ma?) - may i try on this shirt?
    *   **contexts:** clothes shopping, fitting rooms.

10. 试衣间 (shìyījiān)
    *   **pinyin:** shìyījiān
    *   **definition:** fitting room; dressing room
    *   **usage:** a room in a shop where you can try on clothes before you buy them.
    *   **example:** 试衣间在那边。(shìyījiān zài nàbiān.) - the fitting room is over there.
    *   **contexts:** clothes shopping.

## day 30

1.  多少钱 (duōshao qián)
    *   **pinyin:** duōshao qián
    *   **definition:** how much money?; what's the price?
    *   **usage:** used to ask about the cost of an item.
    *   **example:** 请问这个多少钱？(qǐngwèn zhège duōshao qián?) - excuse me, how much is this?
    *   **contexts:** shopping, asking for prices.

2.  价格 (jiàgé)
    *   **pinyin:** jiàgé
    *   **definition:** price
    *   **usage:** the amount of money expected, required, or given in payment for something.
    *   **example:** 这个价格太贵了。(zhège jiàgé tài guì le.) - this price is too expensive.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, commerce, economics.

3.  贵 (guì)
    *   **pinyin:** guì
    *   **definition:** expensive; valuable
    *   **usage:** costing a lot of money.
    *   **example:** 能不能便宜一点？太贵了。(néng bù néng piányi yīdiǎn? tài guì le.) - can it be a bit cheaper? it's too expensive.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, bargaining.

4.  便宜 (piányi)
    *   **pinyin:** piányi
    *   **definition:** cheap; inexpensive
    *   **usage:** low in price; worth more than its cost.
    *   **example:** 这件衣服很便宜。(zhè jiàn yīfu hěn piányi.) - these clothes are very cheap.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, bargaining.

5.  打折 (dǎzhé)
    *   **pinyin:** dǎzhé
    *   **definition:** to give a discount; discount
    *   **usage:** a reduction made from the gross amount or value of something.
    *   **example:** 现在买这件商品可以打八折。(xiànzài mǎi zhè jiàn shāngpǐn kěyǐ dǎ bā zhé.) - you can get a 20% discount if you buy this product now (打八折 means 80% of the original price, i.e., 20% off).
    *   **contexts:** shopping, promotions, sales.

6.  讲价 (jiǎngjià)
    *   **pinyin:** jiǎngjià
    *   **definition:** to bargain; to haggle
    *   **usage:** to discuss the price of something in an attempt to reduce it.
    *   **example:** 在一些小市场可以讲价。(zài yīxiē xiǎo shìchǎng kěyǐ jiǎngjià.) - you can bargain in some small markets.
    *   **contexts:** shopping (especially in markets), negotiation.

7.  付款 (fùkuǎn)
    *   **pinyin:** fùkuǎn
    *   **definition:** to pay (money)
    *   **usage:** to give money in return for goods or services.
    *   **example:** 您想用什么方式付款？(nín xiǎng yòng shénme fāngshì fùkuǎn?) - how would you like to pay?
    *   **contexts:** shopping, transactions, banking.

8.  现金 (xiànjīn)
    *   **pinyin:** xiànjīn
    *   **definition:** cash
    *   **usage:** money in coins or notes, as distinct from checks, money orders, or credit.
    *   **example:** 我可以用现金支付吗？(wǒ kěyǐ yòng xiànjīn zhīfù ma?) - can i pay in cash?
    *   **contexts:** payment methods, finance.

9.  信用卡 (xìnyòngkǎ)
    *   **pinyin:** xìnyòngkǎ
    *   **definition:** credit card
    *   **usage:** a small plastic card issued by a bank, building society, etc., allowing the holder to purchase goods or services on credit.
    *   **example:** 我忘了带信用卡。(wǒ wàng le dài xìnyòngkǎ.) - i forgot to bring my credit card.
    *   **contexts:** payment methods, finance, banking.

10. 支付宝 (zhīfùbǎo)
    *   **pinyin:** zhīfùbǎo
    *   **definition:** alipay (popular chinese mobile payment platform)
    *   **usage:** a widely used mobile and online payment platform in china.
    *   **example:** 你可以用支付宝付款。(nǐ kěyǐ yòng zhīfùbǎo fùkuǎn.) - you can pay with alipay.
    *   **contexts:** mobile payment, e-commerce in china, technology.

## day 31

2.  收银台 (shōuyíntái)
    *   **pinyin:** shōuyíntái
    *   **definition:** cashier; checkout counter
    *   **usage:** the place in a store where payment is made for goods purchased.
    *   **example:** 请到收银台付款。(qǐng dào shōuyíntái fùkuǎn.) - please pay at the cashier.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, retail stores.

3.  小票 (xiǎopiào)
    *   **pinyin:** xiǎopiào
    *   **definition:** receipt (from a purchase, small slip of paper)
    *   **usage:** a small piece of paper confirming that one has paid for or received goods or services.
    *   **example:** 请您收好小票。(qǐng nín shōuhǎo xiǎopiào.) - please keep your receipt carefully.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, proof of purchase, returns.

4.  发票 (fāpiào)
    *   **pinyin:** fāpiào
    *   **definition:** invoice; official receipt (for reimbursement or tax purposes)
    *   **usage:** an official document issued by a seller to a buyer relating to a sale transaction and indicating the products, quantities, and agreed prices for products or services the seller had provided the buyer. used for accounting and reimbursement.
    *   **example:** 我需要一张发票报销。(wǒ xūyào yī zhāng fāpiào bàoxiāo.) - i need an official receipt for reimbursement.
    *   **contexts:** business expenses, accounting, formal transactions.

5.  退货 (tuìhuò)
    *   **pinyin:** tuìhuò
    *   **definition:** to return merchandise; returned goods
    *   **usage:** to give back an item bought to the seller, usually for a refund or exchange.
    *   **example:** 如果不合适，可以在七天内退货。(rúguǒ bù héshì, kěyǐ zài qī tiān nèi tuìhuò.) - if it's not suitable, you can return it within seven days.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, customer service, consumer rights.

6.  换货 (huànhuò)
    *   **pinyin:** huànhuò
    *   **definition:** to exchange merchandise
    *   **usage:** to give back an item bought and receive another item in its place.
    *   **example:** 我想把这件衣服换个大号的。(wǒ xiǎng bǎ zhè jiàn yīfu huàn gè dà hào de.) - i want to exchange this piece of clothing for a larger size.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, customer service.

7.  质量 (zhìliàng)
    *   **pinyin:** zhìliàng
    *   **definition:** quality (of goods)
    *   **usage:** the standard of something as measured against other things of a similar kind; the degree of excellence of something.
    *   **example:** 这件商品的质量很好。(zhè jiàn shāngpǐn de zhìliàng hěn hǎo.) - the quality of this product is very good.
    *   **contexts:** shopping, manufacturing, product reviews.

8.  品牌 (pǐnpái)
    *   **pinyin:** pǐnpái
    *   **definition:** brand; trademark
    *   **usage:** a type of product manufactured by a particular company under a particular name.
    *   **example:** 你喜欢哪个品牌的手机？(nǐ xǐhuān nǎge pǐnpái de shǒujī?) - which brand of mobile phone do you like?
    *   **contexts:** shopping, marketing, consumer goods.

9.  新款 (xīnkuǎn)
    *   **pinyin:** xīnkuǎn
    *   **definition:** new style; latest model
    *   **usage:** a new design or version of a product.
    *   **example:** 这是我们店里最新款的连衣裙。(zhè shì wǒmen diàn lǐ zuì xīnkuǎn de liányīqún.) - this is the newest style of dress in our store.
    *   **contexts:** fashion, technology, shopping.

10. 促销 (cùxiāo)
    *   **pinyin:** cùxiāo
    *   **definition:** sales promotion; to promote sales
    *   **usage:** activity that supports or provides active encouragement for the furtherance of a cause, venture, or aim, especially the sale of a product.
    *   **example:** 商场正在进行年底大促销。(shāngchǎng zhèngzài jìnxíng niándǐ dà cùxiāo.) - the mall is having a big year-end sales promotion.
    *   **contexts:** marketing, retail, shopping.
