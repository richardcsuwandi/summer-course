# 📅 Week 8: Social Interactions & Making Plans

## day 50

1.  朋友 (péngyou)
    *   **pinyin:** péngyou
    *   **definition:** friend
    *   **usage:** a person whom one knows and with whom one has a bond of mutual affection, typically exclusive of sexual or family relations.
    *   **example:** 他是我的好朋友。(tā shì wǒ de hǎo péngyou.) - he is my good friend.
    *   **contexts:** relationships, social life.

2.  认识 (rènshi)
    *   **pinyin:** rènshi
    *   **definition:** to know (someone); to be acquainted with; to recognize
    *   **usage:** to have met someone before and be able to identify them; to have knowledge or experience of.
    *   **example:** 你认识他吗？(nǐ rènshi tā ma?) - do you know him? / 很高兴认识你。(hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ.) - nice to meet you.
    *   **contexts:** introductions, social interactions, relationships.

3.  邀请 (yāoqǐng)
    *   **pinyin:** yāoqǐng
    *   **definition:** to invite; invitation
    *   **usage:** a spoken or written request to someone to go somewhere or to do something.
    *   **example:** 我想邀请你参加我的生日聚会。(wǒ xiǎng yāoqǐng nǐ cānjiā wǒ de shēngrì jùhuì.) - i want to invite you to my birthday party.
    *   **contexts:** social events, making plans, formal requests.

4.  聚会 (jùhuì)
    *   **pinyin:** jùhuì
    *   **definition:** party; gathering; to get together
    *   **usage:** a social event at which a group of people meet to talk, eat, drink, etc.
    *   **example:** 我们周末有个小聚会。(wǒmen zhōumò yǒu gè xiǎo jùhuì.) - we are having a small get-together this weekend.
    *   **contexts:** social life, events, celebrations.

5.  参加 (cānjiā)
    *   **pinyin:** cānjiā
    *   **definition:** to participate; to attend; to join
    *   **usage:** to take part in an activity or event.
    *   **example:** 你会参加明天的会议吗？(nǐ huì cānjiā míngtiān de huìyì ma?) - will you attend tomorrow's meeting?
    *   **contexts:** events, activities, meetings, social gatherings.

6.  见面 (jiànmiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** jiànmiàn
    *   **definition:** to meet; to see (someone)
    *   **usage:** to come into the presence or company of (someone) by chance or arrangement.
    *   **example:** 我们什么时候能见面？(wǒmen shénme shíhou néng jiànmiàn?) - when can we meet?
    *   **contexts:** making plans, social interactions, appointments.

7.  约会 (yuēhuì)
    *   **pinyin:** yuēhuì
    *   **definition:** appointment; date (romantic or social); to make an appointment/date
    *   **usage:** a social or romantic engagement; an arrangement to meet someone at a particular time and place.
    *   **example:** 我今天晚上有个约会。(wǒ jīntiān wǎnshang yǒu gè yuēhuì.) - i have a date tonight. / 我们约个时间吃饭吧。(wǒmen yuē gè shíjiān chīfàn ba.) - let's make an appointment to have a meal.
    *   **contexts:** social plans, romantic relationships, scheduling.

8.  联系 (liánxì)
    *   **pinyin:** liánxì
    *   **definition:** to contact; to get in touch with; connection; relation
    *   **usage:** to communicate with someone, typically to give or pass on information.
    *   **example:** 我们以后常联系。(wǒmen yǐhòu cháng liánxì.) - let's keep in touch. / 请留下您的联系方式。(qǐng liúxià nín de liánxì fāngshì.) - please leave your contact information.
    *   **contexts:** communication, relationships, networking.

9.  聊天 (liáotiān)
    *   **pinyin:** liáotiān
    *   **definition:** to chat; to gossip
    *   **usage:** to talk in a friendly and informal way.
    *   **example:** 我喜欢和朋友聊天。(wǒ xǐhuān hé péngyou liáotiān.) - i like to chat with friends.
    *   **contexts:** social interactions, leisure, communication.

10. 开玩笑 (kāi wánxiào)
    *   **pinyin:** kāi wánxiào
    *   **definition:** to joke; to make fun of; to tease
    *   **usage:** to say something funny or to behave in a way that is intended to amuse.
    *   **example:** 别生气，我只是开个玩笑。(bié shēngqì, wǒ zhǐshì kāi gè wánxiào.) - don't be angry, i was just joking.
    *   **contexts:** humor, social interactions, informal conversation.

## day 51

1.  拜访 (bàifǎng)
    *   **pinyin:** bàifǎng
    *   **definition:** to pay a visit (to someone, more formal)
    *   **usage:** to go to see and spend time with someone socially or as a courtesy.
    *   **example:** 我下周要去拜访一位老教授。(wǒ xiàzhōu yào qù bàifǎng yī wèi lǎo jiàoshòu.) - i am going to pay a visit to an old professor next week.
    *   **contexts:** formal visits, showing respect, professional or academic relations.

2.  做客 (zuòkè)
    *   **pinyin:** zuòkè
    *   **definition:** to be a guest
    *   **usage:** to visit someone's home as a guest.
    *   **example:** 欢迎你来我家做客。(huānyíng nǐ lái wǒjiā zuòkè.) - you are welcome to be a guest at my home.
    *   **contexts:** social visits, hospitality.

3.  款待 (kuǎndài)
    *   **pinyin:** kuǎndài
    *   **definition:** to entertain (guests); to treat cordially
    *   **usage:** to receive and entertain guests or visitors warmly or generously.
    *   **example:** 感谢您的热情款待。(gǎnxiè nín de rèqíng kuǎndài.) - thank you for your warm hospitality.
    *   **contexts:** hosting guests, social etiquette.

4.  礼物 (lǐwù)
    *   **pinyin:** lǐwù
    *   **definition:** gift; present
    *   **usage:** a thing given willingly to someone without payment; a present.
    *   **example:** 我给他准备了一份生日礼物。(wǒ gěi tā zhǔnbèi le yī fèn shēngrì lǐwù.) - i prepared a birthday gift for him.
    *   **contexts:** celebrations, social customs, expressing gratitude or affection.

5.  送礼物 (sòng lǐwù)
    *   **pinyin:** sòng lǐwù
    *   **definition:** to give a gift
    *   **usage:** the act of presenting a gift to someone.
    *   **example:** 在中国，过年的时候长辈会给晚辈送礼物（红包）。(zài zhōngguó, guònián de shíhou zhǎngbèi huì gěi wǎnbèi sòng lǐwù (hóngbāo).) - in china, elders give gifts (red envelopes) to younger generations during chinese new year.
    *   **contexts:** celebrations, gift-giving etiquette.

6.  打扰 (dǎrǎo)
    *   **pinyin:** dǎrǎo
    *   **definition:** to disturb; to trouble; to bother
    *   **usage:** to interrupt the peace, quiet, or rest of (someone); to inconvenience.
    *   **example:** 对不起，打扰一下。请问洗手间在哪里？(duìbuqǐ, dǎrǎo yīxià. qǐngwèn xǐshǒujiān zài nǎlǐ?) - sorry to bother you. excuse me, where is the restroom?
    *   **contexts:** apologies, asking for help, politeness.

7.  抱歉 (bàoqiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** bàoqiàn
    *   **definition:** to be sorry; to regret; apology
    *   **usage:** feeling or expressing regret or penitence; a regretful acknowledgment of an offense or failure.
    *   **example:** 非常抱歉，我迟到了。(fēicháng bàoqiàn, wǒ chídào le.) - i am very sorry, i am late.
    *   **contexts:** apologies, expressing regret, social etiquette.

8.  没关系 (méi guānxi)
    *   **pinyin:** méi guānxi
    *   **definition:** it doesn't matter; it's okay; never mind
    *   **usage:** used to say that something is not important or that one is not angry or upset.
    *   **example:** A: 对不起！ B: 没关系。(A: duìbuqǐ! B: méi guānxi.) - A: sorry! B: it's okay.
    *   **contexts:** responding to apologies, reassurance.

9.  麻烦 (máfan)
    *   **pinyin:** máfan
    *   **definition:** troublesome; inconvenient; to trouble someone; trouble (noun)
    *   **usage:** causing difficulty or annoyance; to cause someone difficulty or inconvenience.
    *   **example:** 麻烦你了，能帮我一下吗？(máfan nǐ le, néng bāng wǒ yīxià ma?) - sorry to trouble you, could you help me? / 这件事真麻烦。(zhè jiàn shì zhēn máfan.) - this matter is really troublesome.
    *   **contexts:** asking for help, expressing difficulty, apologies.

10. 感谢 (gǎnxiè)
    *   **pinyin:** gǎnxiè
    *   **definition:** to thank; to be grateful; thanks (noun)
    *   **usage:** to express gratitude to (someone), especially by saying "thank you."
    *   **example:** 非常感谢您的帮助。(fēicháng gǎnxiè nín de bāngzhù.) - thank you very much for your help.
    *   **contexts:** expressing gratitude, social etiquette, politeness.

## day 52

1.  不客气 (bú kèqi)
    *   **pinyin:** bú kèqi
    *   **definition:** you're welcome; don't be polite
    *   **usage:** a polite response to an expression of thanks.
    *   **example:** A: 谢谢你！ B: 不客气。(A: xièxie nǐ! B: bú kèqi.) - A: thank you! B: you're welcome.
    *   **contexts:** responding to thanks, politeness.

2.  请客 (qǐngkè)
    *   **pinyin:** qǐngkè
    *   **definition:** to treat someone (to a meal, drink, etc.); to act as host
    *   **usage:** to pay for someone else's food or drink, especially as a way of being hospitable or celebrating something.
    *   **example:** 今天我请客，随便点！(jīntiān wǒ qǐngkè, suíbiàn diǎn!) - it's my treat today, order whatever you like!
    *   **contexts:** social dining, celebrations, hospitality.

3.  AA制 (AA zhì)
    *   **pinyin:** AA zhì
    *   **definition:** to go dutch; to split the bill (AA system)
    *   **usage:** a system where each person pays for their own share of a bill, especially for a meal.
    *   **example:** 我们吃饭通常是AA制。(wǒmen chīfàn tōngcháng shì AA zhì.) - we usually go dutch when we eat out.
    *   **contexts:** social dining, paying bills, group outings.

4.  庆祝 (qìngzhù)
    *   **pinyin:** qìngzhù
    *   **definition:** to celebrate
    *   **usage:** to acknowledge (a significant or happy day or event) with a social gathering or enjoyable activity.
    *   **example:** 我们要去庆祝他的生日。(wǒmen yào qù qìngzhù tā de shēngrì.) - we are going to celebrate his birthday.
    *   **contexts:** special occasions, parties, holidays.

5.  生日快乐 (shēngrì kuàilè)
    *   **pinyin:** shēngrì kuàilè
    *   **definition:** happy birthday
    *   **usage:** a conventional greeting or expression of good wishes on someone's birthday.
    *   **example:** 祝你生日快乐！(zhù nǐ shēngrì kuàilè!) - wish you a happy birthday!
    *   **contexts:** birthdays, celebrations.

6.  节日快乐 (jiérì kuàilè)
    *   **pinyin:** jiérì kuàilè
    *   **definition:** happy holidays
    *   **usage:** a general greeting used during holiday seasons.
    *   **example:** 祝大家节日快乐！(zhù dàjiā jiérì kuàilè!) - happy holidays to everyone!
    *   **contexts:** holidays, festivals, seasonal greetings.

7.  新年快乐 (xīnnián kuàilè)
    *   **pinyin:** xīnnián kuàilè
    *   **definition:** happy new year
    *   **usage:** a greeting used at the beginning of a new year.
    *   **example:** 新年快乐，恭喜发财！(xīnnián kuàilè, gōngxǐ fācái!) - happy new year, may you be prosperous!
    *   **contexts:** new year celebrations (both solar and lunar).

8.  恭喜 (gōngxǐ)
    *   **pinyin:** gōngxǐ
    *   **definition:** congratulations; to congratulate
    *   **usage:** an expression of pleasure or approval at someone's success or good fortune.
    *   **example:** 恭喜你通过了考试！(gōngxǐ nǐ tōngguò le kǎoshì!) - congratulations on passing the exam!
    *   **contexts:** achievements, good news, celebrations.

9.  干杯 (gānbēi)
    *   **pinyin:** gānbēi
    *   **definition:** cheers!; to drink a toast (literally: dry cup)
    *   **usage:** a call to drink in honor of a person or event.
    *   **example:** 为我们的友谊干杯！(wèi wǒmen de yǒuyì gānbēi!) - cheers to our friendship!
    *   **contexts:** celebrations, dining, social drinking.

10. 随便 (suíbiàn)
    *   **pinyin:** suíbiàn
    *   **definition:** as one pleases; casual; random; informal
    *   **usage:** in a free and unrestricted way; not formal or official.
    *   **example:** 你想吃什么？随便点。(nǐ xiǎng chī shénme? suíbiàn diǎn.) - what do you want to eat? order whatever you like. / 他说话很随便。(tā shuōhuà hěn suíbiàn.) - he speaks very casually.
    *   **contexts:** making choices, informal situations, behavior.

## day 53

1.  建议 (jiànyì)
    *   **pinyin:** jiànyì
    *   **definition:** to suggest; suggestion; proposal
    *   **usage:** an idea or plan put forward for consideration.
    *   **example:** 我有个建议，我们明天去看电影怎么样？(wǒ yǒu gè jiànyì, wǒmen míngtiān qù kàn diànyǐng zěnmeyàng?) - i have a suggestion, how about we go watch a movie tomorrow?
    *   **contexts:** making plans, problem-solving, giving advice.

2.  同意 (tóngyì)
    *   **pinyin:** tóngyì
    *   **definition:** to agree; to consent; to approve
    *   **usage:** to have the same opinion about something; to give permission for something to happen.
    *   **example:** 我同意你的看法。(wǒ tóngyì nǐ de kànfǎ.) - i agree with your opinion.
    *   **contexts:** discussions, decision-making, making plans.

3.  反对 (fǎnduì)
    *   **pinyin:** fǎnduì
    *   **definition:** to oppose; to object to; against
    *   **usage:** to express disapproval of or disagreement with (something).
    *   **example:** 我反对这个计划，因为它风险太大了。(wǒ fǎnduì zhège jìhuà, yīnwèi tā fēngxiǎn tài dà le.) - i oppose this plan because its risks are too high.
    *   **contexts:** discussions, debates, decision-making.

4.  决定 (juédìng)
    *   **pinyin:** juédìng
    *   **definition:** to decide; decision
    *   **usage:** a conclusion or resolution reached after consideration.
    *   **example:** 我们决定下周去旅行。(wǒmen juédìng xiàzhōu qù lǚxíng.) - we decided to travel next week.
    *   **contexts:** making plans, problem-solving, important choices.

5.  当然 (dāngrán)
    *   **pinyin:** dāngrán
    *   **definition:** of course; certainly; without doubt
    *   **usage:** used to express emphatic agreement or affirmation.
    *   **example:** A: 你能帮我吗？ B: 当然可以。(A: nǐ néng bāng wǒ ma? B: dāngrán kěyǐ.) - A: can you help me? B: of course, i can.
    *   **contexts:** expressing certainty, agreeing, responding.

6.  也许 (yěxǔ)
    *   **pinyin:** yěxǔ
    *   **definition:** perhaps; maybe; probably
    *   **usage:** used to express uncertainty or possibility.
    *   **example:** 他也许会来，也许不会来。(tā yěxǔ huì lái, yěxǔ bù huì lái.) - maybe he will come, maybe he won't.
    *   **contexts:** expressing uncertainty, possibilities.

7.  可能 (kěnéng)
    *   **pinyin:** kěnéng
    *   **definition:** possible; maybe; possibility; may
    *   **usage:** that can exist, happen, or be done; perhaps.
    *   **example:** 明天可能会下雨。(míngtiān kěnéng huì xiàyǔ.) - it might rain tomorrow.
    *   **contexts:** expressing possibility, future events, uncertainty.

8.  一定 (yídìng)
    *   **pinyin:** yídìng
    *   **definition:** definitely; certainly; surely; must
    *   **usage:** without any doubt; used to emphasize that something is true or will happen.
    *   **example:** 我一定会尽力帮助你。(wǒ yídìng huì jìnlì bāngzhù nǐ.) - i will definitely do my best to help you.
    *   **contexts:** expressing certainty, making promises, giving assurance.

9.  改天 (gǎitiān)
    *   **pinyin:** gǎitiān
    *   **definition:** some other day; another day
    *   **usage:** used to postpone something to an unspecified day in the future.
    *   **example:** 我今天没空，我们改天再约吧。(wǒ jīntiān méi kòng, wǒmen gǎitiān zài yuē ba.) - i am not free today, let's meet some other day.
    *   **contexts:** postponing plans, scheduling.

10. 下次 (xiàcì)
    *   **pinyin:** xiàcì
    *   **definition:** next time
    *   **usage:** the next occasion or instance.
    *   **example:** 这次不行，下次吧。(zhècì bù xíng, xiàcì ba.) - not this time, maybe next time.
    *   **contexts:** making future plans, postponing.

9.  习惯 (xíguàn)

    *   **usage:** the variation in beliefs, behaviors, and practices that exist between different cultures.

    *   **contexts:** intercultural communication, sociology, travel.

## day 55

1.  风俗 (fēngsú)
    *   **pinyin:** fēngsú
    *   **definition:** custom; social custom
    *   **usage:** a traditional and widely accepted way of behaving or doing something that is specific to a particular society, place, or time.
    *   **example:** 每个国家都有不同的风俗习惯。(měi gè guójiā dōu yǒu bùtóng de fēngsú xíguàn.) - every country has different customs and habits.
    *   **contexts:** culture, traditions, sociology, anthropology.

2.  传统 (chuántǒng)
    *   **pinyin:** chuántǒng
    *   **definition:** tradition; traditional
    *   **usage:** the transmission of customs or beliefs from generation to generation, or the fact of being passed on in this way.
    *   **example:** 春节是中国最重要的传统节日。(chūnjié shì zhōngguó zuì zhòngyào de chuántǒng jiérì.) - the spring festival is the most important traditional festival in china.
    *   **contexts:** culture, history, festivals, values.

3.  误会 (wùhuì)
    *   **pinyin:** wùhuì
    *   **definition:** misunderstanding; to misunderstand
    *   **usage:** a failure to understand something correctly.
    *   **example:** 对不起，这可能是一场误会。(duìbuqǐ, zhè kěnéng shì yī chǎng wùhuì.) - sorry, this might be a misunderstanding.
    *   **contexts:** communication, relationships, problem-solving.

4.  解释 (jiěshì)
    *   **pinyin:** jiěshì
    *   **definition:** to explain; explanation
    *   **usage:** to make (an idea, situation, or problem) clear to someone by describing it in more detail or revealing relevant facts or ideas.
    *   **example:** 请你解释一下这是怎么回事。(qǐng nǐ jiěshì yīxià zhè shì zěnme huíshì.) - please explain what happened.
    *   **contexts:** communication, problem-solving, clarification.

5.  意见 (yìjiàn)
